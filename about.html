About Me

Hello!

My name is Eunice Choi, and I am a San Francisco Bobolink at Dev Bootcamp. I accidentally fell in love with programming when I took an Intro Python course during my senior year at UCLA. Ever since, I have been working my way to become a full stack web developer.

I look forward to improving and adding content to this site as I learn new languages and frameworks. Thanks for visiting, and feel free to look around!
